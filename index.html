Starboi

"Starboy" is a song by Canadian singer The Weeknd featuring French electronic duo Daft Punk, released as the lead single from The Weeknd's third studio album of the same name on September 22, 2016.

The artists co-wrote and co-produced the song with Doc McKinney and Henry Walter, with additional writing from Jason Quenneville.

It was released as the first single from the album on September 22, 2016, by XO and Republic Records.
